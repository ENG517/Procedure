**User scenarios**:

## Scenario 1: Liza, the international student

- **Who**: Liza, an international student, unfamiliar with ChatGPT.
- **Motivation**: Liza wants to see if ChatGPT can help correct her grammar mistakes and assist in writing long, formal emails with the correct structure.
- **Goal**: She wants to avoid grammar mistakes in her English writing, particularly when emailing professors or communicating in a professional academic setting.
- **Important characteristics**: 
  - Liza is looking for straightforward guidance on how to write clear and effective prompts in English.

## Scenario 2: Ben, the marketing professional

- **Who**: Ben, a marketing professional who has used ChatGPT a few times as a search engine but has never utilized it for content generation tasks.
- **Motivation**: Ben needs to create weekly marketing reports and generate social media content. He has used ChatGPT for brainstorming but often receives generic responses and now seeks more targeted, relevant outputs to enhance his efficiency.
- **Goal**: Ben wants to improve his prompt-writing skills to have ChatGPT generate more detailed and relevant content, tailored to his specific needs.
- **Important characteristics**:
  - Ben understands the basics of ChatGPT but needs guidance on refining his prompts for professional use, including how to make the AI familiar with his niche, provide context or references, and generate more precise results.

## Scenario 3: Maria, the AI beginner

- **Who**: Maria, a middle-aged woman who is new to using AI tools like ChatGPT.
- **Motivation**: Maria has heard about the benefits of AI from her colleagues and wants to reduce the time spent on daily writing tasks.
- **Goal**: She wants to learn how to use ChatGPT for automating tasks like writing meeting summaries, generating reports, and handling other routine professional writing tasks.
- **Important characteristics**:
  - Maria has limited knowledge of AI tools and is looking for practical, step-by-step instructions.

## Scenario 4: James, the middle school student

- **Who**: James, a middle school student, who wants to use ChatGPT for educational purposes and has heard about this tool from his classmates. He wants it to explain the topic that he didn’t get in school.
- **Motivation**: James wants to better understand topics he struggles with in school.
- **Goal**: James wants to receive a simplified explanation and examples that will help him understand a new topic.
- **Important Characteristics**: 
  - James is 14 years old. He needs guidance that is written in a simple manner.
