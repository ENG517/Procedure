## About BLUP
The Best Linear Unbiased Prediction value, commonly referred to as BLUP, is a genetic index for horses. It plays a role in breeding because the BLUP values of the parent horses impact the foal's genetic potential and skills at birth.

The BLUP value for each horse falls within a range between -100 and 100, with -100 being the lowest possible BLUP and 100 being the highest possible BLUP that a horse can reach.

A higher average BLUP of the parents will increase the foal's chance of having a higher genetic potential and skills at birth, or innate skills. The foal will not have any innate skills if the average BLUP of the parents is below 0.

Many factors influence a horse's BLUP, including: training level in the best three skills for that breed, number of competitions won, age.

**To achieve a BLUP value of 100 for a horse, your horse must:**
- Obtain the maximium possible skill points in the best three skills for its breed
- Win at least 20 competitions
- Be at least 10 years of age
