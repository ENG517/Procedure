# Reserving and applying an affix to a horse

## About affixes
Affixes are like the surnames of a horse. On Howrse, they appear beneath a horse's name in italics and hyperlink to a page that displays a list of all the horses that have been assigned that affix. Breeders apply affixes to horses as a way to identify and market different bloodlines in the game.

## Prerequisites
Before reserving and applying an affix to a horse, you'll need at least 30 days of seniority. You may reserve one affix for every 30 days of seniority on the game; this is cumulative.

> Note: Affixes can only be reserved if the name chosen for the affix is not already in use by another breeder.

Horses that you choose to assign an affix to must:
- Be born in your breeding farm
- Not be part of a breeding team's lineage

> Note: A horse can only receive up to **one** affix. If a horse was born in your breeding farm, you may change its affix at any time. If a horse was not born in your breeding farm, you cannot change or remove any affix that its breeder assigned to it.
