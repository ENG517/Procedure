# How to Make Peanut Butter Bars

Need to make a quick-to-prep dessert with stuff you (probably) already have? Not great at baking, but still want to impress? Here is a recipe for Peanut Butter Bars, an easy-to-make dessert using things you may already have. To start you'll need the following: <!--Just a little thing. ## Ingredients
**For the Base**

* 50g of muscovado sugar
  * *If you can't find muscovado, use dark brown sugar instead.*
* 200g icing sugar (aka powdered sugar) 
* 50g unsalted butter
* 200g smooth peanut butter

![ingredients for the top](assets\images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-top-i.jpg)

**For the topping**

* 200g milk chocolate chips
* 100g dark chocoalate chips
* 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

![ingredients for the bottom](assets\images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-bottom-i.jpg)

## Tools and Utensils
* A fridge
* A square brownie pan 
* Some mixing spoons
* Measuring spoons
* A scale
* A big bowl for mixing
* A microwave-safe bowl
* Aluminum foil

## Steps

1. Gather all of your ingredients and measure them out using the scale and measuring spoons.
2. Stir all of the base  ingredients together in the big mixing bowl.

   *  *Don’t worry too much about small clumps of muscovado sugar, it is normal and will happen. It adds to the flavor.*
  ![image of step 2](assets\images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-i2.jpg)
1. Pour and mash the base into brownie pan.
   * *The mixture will be sandy and somewhat malleable.*
  ![ingredients for the top](assets\images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-i3.jpg)
2. Melt butter and chocolate together in the microwave in the microwave-safe bowl. Remove the bowl with the mixture every 10 seconds or so to stir.
   * *The mixture will still be thick when ready and probably take under two minutes*
   * *Watch out during this step for burning.*
  ![ingredients for the top](assets\images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-i4.jpg)
3. Pour the melted chocolate on top of the base and smooth out.
4. Cover the pan with some aluminum foil and place into the fridge for several hours.
![ingredients for the top](assets\images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-i5.jpg)
5. Cut into squares when hardened after a few hours. 
![ingredients for the top](assets\images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-images\p-make-peanutbutter-bars-i6.jpg)
6. Eat!
