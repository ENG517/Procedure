# How to Make Peanut Butter Bars

Need to make a quick-to-prep dessert with stuff you (probably) already have? Not great at baking, but still want to impress? Here is a recipe for Peanut Butter Bars, an easy-to-make dessert using things you may already have. To start you'll need the following:

## Ingredients
**For the Base**

* 50g of dark brown sugar (or muscovado sugar if you can find it)
* 200g icing sugar (aka powdered sugar) 
* 50g unsalted butter
* 200g smooth peanut butter

**For the topping**

* 200g milk chocolate chips
* 100g dark chocoalate chips
* 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

## Tools and Utensils
* A fridge
* A square brownie pan 
* Some mixing spoons
* Measuring spoons
* A scale
* A big bowl for mixing
* A microwave safe bowl
* Aluminum foil

## Steps

1. Gather all of your ingredients and measure them out using the scale and measuring spoons
2. Stir all of the base  ingredients together

   *  Don’t worry too much about small clumps of muscovado sugar 
3. Pour/mash the base into brownie pan 
   * The mixture will be sandy and somewhat malleable
4. Melt butter and chocolate together in the microwave. Remove the bowl with the mixture every 10 seconds or so to stir
   * The mixture will still be thick when ready
5. Pour the melted chocolate on top of base and smooth out
6. Cover the tin with some aluminum foil and place into the fridge
7. Cut into squares when hardened after a few hours. 
8. Eat!
