# How to Reserve a Room at One of NCSU's Libraries

Want to find better places to study and get work done on campus? New to NCSU and want to learn about what sort of resources NCSU provides it's students? Here, you can learn how to reserve a room in one of NCSU's libraries, a great resource for getting a quiet place for getting work done, hosting meetings or study groups, and more. All you need to get started is a laptop or computer, access to wifi, your mypack login credentials and student ID card if you're a graduate student reserving a graduate room. 

## Instructions

1. Go to the NCSU libraries website
   ![image for step 1](assets\p-ncsu-libraries-reserve-room-images\p-ncsu-libraries-reserve-room-i1.png)
2. Hover over the “study and learn” button on the top menu bar
 3. Go down and select “reserve a room” under “spaces”![image for step 2 and 3](assets\p-ncsu-libraries-reserve-room-images\p-ncsu-libraries-reserve-room-i2.png)
4. Log in with your NCSU student log in
5. Select what kind of space you want from the drop down on the left (circled in red)
6. Select what building you want from the drop down on the right (circled in green)
7. Select the day you want to reserve for (circled in blue)
 ![image for step 5, 6 and 7](assets\p-ncsu-libraries-reserve-room-images\p-ncsu-libraries-reserve-room-i3.png)
8. Click “update”
9.  Scroll down and find a room you like the look of that has slots open for  when you want to use the room
10. Click on the time slot you want to begin reserving the room for (it will look like a yellow vertical rectangle)
 ![image for step 10](assets\p-ncsu-libraries-reserve-room-images\p-ncsu-libraries-reserve-room-i4.png)
11. Enter the description in the title (keep it simple)
12. Select the end time you want (you are limited to 2 hour slots, but you can make a couple reservations in a row)
 ![image for step 11 and 12](assets\p-ncsu-libraries-reserve-room-images\p-ncsu-libraries-reserve-room-i5.png)
13. Click “Create reservation” and you're done!
14. Make additional reservations if you would like by repeating steps 5-13
