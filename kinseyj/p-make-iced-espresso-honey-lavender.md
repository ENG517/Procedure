# How to Make an Iced Honey Lavender Shaken Espresso

Want a new espresso recipe to make at home that's cheaper than a certain coffee chain and just as good, if not better? Here is a recipe for a delicious iced shaken espresso recipe with an amazing flavor combination.

## Utentils  and Supplies To Get Started

* An espresso maker of some kind
* Three glasses 
    * One for frothing your espresso, one for frothing your half and half, and one to make your espresso into
* A milk frother, preferably a handheld one
* A straw
* A spoon
* A fridge with a freezer

## Ingredients 

* Half and Half (can be substituted for some other milk substitute)
* Espresso pods for your espresso maker
* Ice
* Lavender flavoring (I use paste)
* Honey

## Instructions

1. Place the milk cup into the freezer
1. Place a  teaspoon of honey into the espresso cup
1. Place an espresso pod into the espresso machine and make the espresso, into the espresso cup with the honey.
1. Mix the coffee a little
1. Leave both the espresso cup to cool down and the milk cup to get cold for about 5-10 mins
    * If you don't do this, you run the chance of your honey not melting into the espresso all the way, *and* of the espresso being too hot and melting the ice, resulting in watered down coffee
2. Come back and pour the espresso into your espresso forthing cup. Use the milk frother to froth the espresso. Move the frother up and down slowly and around the cup, do this for about 1 minute.
3. Take your milk cup out of the freezer, pour half and half or milk into it. Usually just enough to cover one half to 1 cm of the bottom of the cup.
4. Add some lavender flavoring to the half and half or milk.
1. Froth with the frother the same way you did the coffee. 
1. Add ice when finished frothing
1. Pour the frothed coffee into the half and half/milk cup. 
1. Add a straw and mix around a little
1. Enjoy!
