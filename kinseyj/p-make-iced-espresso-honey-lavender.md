# How to Make an Iced Honey Lavender Shaken Espresso

Want a new espresso recipe to make at home that's cheaper than a certain coffee chain and just as good, if not better? Here is a recipe for a delicious iced shaken espresso recipe with an amazing flavor combination. You can expect this entire process to take around 20 minutes. 

## Utentils and Supplies To Get Started

* An espresso maker of some kind
  * *for example, I use a "Mixpresso", which is a knock-off of the popular "Nespresso" machine. You could also use a classic espresso machine (often used in cafes and restaurants). Anything that makes espresso will work, just make sure to follow your individual models instructions.*
* Three glasses 
    * *One for frothing your espresso, one for frothing your half and half, and one to make your espresso into.*
* A milk frother, preferably a handheld one
* A straw
* A spoon
* A fridge with a freezer

## Ingredients 

* Half-and-Half 
  * *can be substituted for some other milk substitute, oat milk would work well for this recipe.*
* Espresso pods for your espresso maker
* Ice
* Lavender flavoring 
  * *I use paste, but drops or syrup could be used.*
* Honey

![Ingredients for making espresso](assets\images\p-make-espresso-images\p-make-espresso-i1.jpg)

## Instructions

### Prep

1. Place the milk cup into the freezer.
2. Place a  teaspoon of honey into the espresso cup.
![step 2 for making espresso](assets\images\p-make-espresso-images\p-make-espresso-i2.jpg)
3. Place an espresso pod into the espresso machine and make the espresso, into the espresso cup with the honey.
![step 3 for making espresso](assets\images\p-make-espresso-images\p-make-espresso-i3.jpg)
4. Mix the coffee a little until the honey is dissolved.
5. Leave both the espresso cup to cool down and the milk cup to get cold for about 5-10 mins.
