Microsoft PowerPoint includes a Speaker Coach tool to improve your presentation skills. Speaker Coach helps you evaluate your pacing, pitch, use of filler words or informal speech, culturally sensitive terms, detects wordiness, or if you are reading you slide word for word via a recording of the presentation. 

**Prerequisites**

<!-- Speaker Coach needs permission to share the screen and microphone to work.
