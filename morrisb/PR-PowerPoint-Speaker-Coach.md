IT Trainers conduct training classes during the year. It is a goal of the department to offer quality content and classes that aid employees in their job tasks.

IT Trainers want employees to continue to sign up for future courses and be looked at as a leader and supporter of technology. Reputation is important for the IT department.


### User Scenario

This is a tool that can help support presentation skills growth. 

IT Training employees conduct classes and those trainers need to practice to better their presentation skills and be more audience oriented. They have to make sure they are time efficient and speak to an audience who are at different levels of technology skills. 

The Rehearsal Coach is an online tool that can offer feedback when used during a practice session. This tool can help the speaker adjust their vocabulary and improve their delivery. 

**Scenario 1**
New trainer to IT Department wants make sure they are teaching to the audience and not overloading their content with jargon employees outside of the IT field would not easily understand.

Their goal is to clearly teach the topic.

The important characteristics is that they realize the need to be inclusive in their words and keep it at a level a beginner can understand. 

**Scenario 2** 
IT trainer is tasked with a performance goal of completing at least 3 classes for employees. To do this they must develop classes that are of interest and need and that students would sign up for because they have had a good learning experience in the past. 

The goal is to create a learning environment that encourages engagement and repeat enrollment. 

The important characteristics of this is the need to understand the level that they are teaching at, practice, and use techniques that engage the students. 

**Scenario 3**
IT trainer is tasked with delivering information to employees that touches on mitigation and risks involved with using AI in a way that is not a barrier but supportive.

The goal: using appropriate language to be a resource and guide and not a barrier or stop point. 

Characteristics: Using the Rehearsal Coach to help fine tune language used during their presentation.

**Scenario 4**
City leadership tasks IT with leading presentations on AI and how the city is using AI to community members. 

The goal is to share facts and use cases with the community using language and identifying concerns they may have. 

Characteristics: Using the Research Coact allows practice with presentations and provides feedback. One example of using a form of AI to support work.
