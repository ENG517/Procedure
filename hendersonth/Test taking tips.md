# Hi Set exam tips
![pencil and paper still](/assets/images/OIP.jpeg)
<ul>
<li> Take 30 minuets to study your study guide for two weeks leading up to the exam, however you must also come to class </li>
<li> 70% of the questions are skills based questions meaning the answers can be found within reading passages, pictures and diagrams </li>
<li>Vocabulary is a crucial part of the test, look through the vocabulary section of your study guide as part as your preparation. However if you are unsure about vocabulary during the test, it is possible to use context clues to find the meaning of the unkown word and possibly the answer. Finally, when answering questions don't just use a word because you are familar with it if it does not answer the question </li>
<li> Answer every question on the test, wrong answers do not count aganist you but skipped questions means there is no chance of procuring a point. If random guessing must occur, choose the letter C.  </li>
</ul>
