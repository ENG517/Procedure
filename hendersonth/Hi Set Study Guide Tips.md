# How to read Hi-Set Subject Study Guides

Welcome to the Hi-Set Subject Study Guide at our school, in total there are six subject study guides made with your needs in mind. If you use this guide step by step you will have a useful tool that can be used at all times to use for your test preparation.
<ol>
<li> Before Starting, look at the table of contents, in the future, this will help you quickly find the sections that you are having difficulties with or otherwise in need of review. The first section are the guides are called <strong> pictorial guides </strong> because of their emphasis on diagrams and illustrations </li>
<li> Go through each of the pictorial guides at least once. It is suggested you follow the order of the table of contents. <i> If you are confused about a word or an idea, please circle the word, draw a star on the page and move on.</i></li>
<li>After reviewing each pictorial guide, you will notice there is a vocabulary section. Vocabulary sections are always after the pictoral guides and are sometimes divided by subject. Review vocabulary carefully. Go back through the pictorial guides to see if your circled words are in the vocabulary section. If you cannot find all of your circled words please consult your instructor </li>
<li>After reviewing each pictorial guide and the vocabulary please proceed to the practice questions. If possible try to spend no more than 80 seconds solving each question. When you are ready please show your answers to  your instructor who will be ready to assist you.</li>

Congratulations for taking this additional step to succesfully pass the Hi-Set exams and achevie success in your educational journey!
</ol>
