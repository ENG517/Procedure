# How to Mark Evidence in Text
## Goal
* Find and mark important evidence in any text
* This is an important skill which will help you with tasks like finding the main idea, and words in context
* Understand key points  
* Connect main ideas

## Materials Needed
* Paper
* Pen or pencil 
* Note paper for mapping ideas

## Step 1: First Reading
* Read without marking
* Get general idea
* Notice paragraph structure

## Step 2: Second Reading with Marks
* Circle key terms
* Underline evidence
* Draw arrows between connected ideas
* Put brackets around main ideas

## Step 3: Make Margin Notes
* Write "M" for main ideas
* Write "E" for examples
* Draw arrows to connect ideas
* Add "?" for unclear points

## Step 4: Check Your Understanding
* Can you explain main points?
* Did you find key evidence?
* Are ideas connected?
* Does everything marked serve a purpose?

## Tips for Success
* Don't mark everything
* Use consistent marking system
* Review your marks for meaning
* Make sure you can explain why each mark is important

> Remember: Your marks should help you understand the text better!
