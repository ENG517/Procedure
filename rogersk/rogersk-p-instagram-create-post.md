# How to make posts on Instagram

This section will walk you through how to make a basic post on Instagram. This is one of the most common forms of content on instagram often called a "grid post" because it stays on your profile, unlike stories, which disappear after 24 hrs. 

Before reading this article, you should already have an Instagram account set up.

Note: This page does not cover stories, reels or going live.

## Posting to your grid
1. On the bottom navigation bar, select the + icon
2. Here, you should see available photos to select from your camera roll

Note, depending on your phone's privacy settings, you may need to select "manage." From there, select which photos you want Instagram to have access to then click "done."

3. From your photos available to instagram, select the photo you want to post. When you have selected it, you should see it appear in the large box at the top of the screen. Then click "next"

Selecting multiple photos: You may wish to post multiple photos within one post. If so, click the square icon so that it lights up in blue. Then select the photos you want to include

Note: The photos will appear in your post in the numbered order you select at this stage

4. Next you can choose to edit your post with a filter, add a song, or add text using the tools in the top right corner. If you wish to skip this step, click "next."
5. Next, you can choose to add a caption, add music, choose your audience, and add location to your post
6. When you're ready to publish your post, click "share"
7. If you want to wait to post click "Save draft."
