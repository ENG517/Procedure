# User Scenario - Create Account and Respond to Post

**Scenario**: 


Scenario 1 - How to Create an Account

Who: Gina is a 50 year old woman who is inspired to make an instagram account to stay in touch with her kids while they are away at college. Gina hasn't used social media until this point and wants to know how to set it up. Although she is familiar with her iphone, she wants extra guidance in properly setting up her account.

Scenario 3 - How to interact with a post

Paul is a new instagram user who, in the past, has only used instagram to post his own content. Paul is a bird photographer who has used instagram as a bird-spotting journal, but want to now use instagram to build relationships with other birders. Paul wants to be able to like, comment, and share content with like-minded people.
