# User Scenario - Title Here

**Scenario for "LinkedIn-Become-Company-Admin" procedure**: 

- Jane is the new Communication Specialist for Marlier Inc. Prior to her hire, the Administrative Specialist for the office (John) has been running the company LinkedIn page (posting, liking, commenting, etc). Now that Jane is hired, the responsibility of managing the page is hers. However, before she can start to do her work, she needs to join the company on LinkedIn as an administrator.

**Scenario for "LinkedIn-Welcome-Post" procedure:**

- With the help of John, Jane has successfully become a page administrator for Marlier Inc.'s company LinkedIn account. Jane's boss has asked that her first act as administrator is to create a "welcome post" that introduces you to the company's LinkedIn followers.
