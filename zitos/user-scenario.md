# User Scenarios - NC State Laundry Machines

**Scenario 1**: Elena is a new NC State student moving into a dormitory for the first time. She has done her laundry before 
at her home, but is unfamiliar with the university’s machines and using a shared laundry room. She wants to clean her 
clothes in the dorm's laundry room.

**Scenario 2**: Marina is a second-year student moving into her apartment at NC State. Her apartment has an in-unit washer 
and dryer. Marina has never washed her own laundry before, and in the past took her clothes back home for her parents 
to clean. She wants to learn how to do her own laundry.
