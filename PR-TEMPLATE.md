- Procedure's Goal:

### Share your screen during a Teams meeting
This is a tool for students who are taking an online class. This tool allows for virtual meetings where students deliver presentations. The presentation requirement also includes a slideshow. Students must know how to share their screen to share their slideshows during the Teams meeting. 

The Teams online sessions are live and students are graded on their participation. 

Sharing their screen is a way to share their work at developing their slideshow. 

## User Scenario

- Potential user: undergrad college student taking a public speaking course
- New user (someone who's getting started with the product)
- Intermediate user (someone who has used the product before)

**Scenario 1**
This is the college student’s first online course and working in Teams. 

The goal is to help the student become comfortable with technology as they move through the semester. 

Characteristics: A new user, who is learning technology while also keeping up with course requirements. 

**Scenario 2** 
The student is having issues with the screenshare feature on Teams.

The goal is to troubleshoot the issue so the student can share their screen. 

Characteristics: the user has other settings on the computer that need to be addressed to allow Teams to share the screen. This is outside of the Teams directions but a step needed and noted in notes to allow for the screenshare.

**Scenario 3**
The student is comfortable with Teams but has never shared their screen.

The goal is to help the student learn more about the tools Teams provides. 

Characteristics: This guide is the direct process this student can use to share their screen.

**Scenario 4** 
The student only has access to a smart phone and needs to be online using the smartphone

The goal is to help them navigate sharing the screen on the desktop and in the app versions of Teams. 

Characteristics: this will include different viewpoints as it is based on a smartphone. Users will have to be comfortable navigating a meeting and sharing a screen on a smartphone device.
