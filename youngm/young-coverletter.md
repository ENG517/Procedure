# How to Write A Cover Letter

As the job market changes, cover letters are becoming less and less common but if a job requires you to submit one alongside your resume - here’s how to get started. 

A cover letter is your opportunity to address the company and to make a good impression, it is also a space where you can explain any personal details you would like them to know, or explain any parts of your resume. But mainly, it should express your interest in the job.

A good template to start with:

- Your Name & Contact Information
- Opening Salutation: Dear, ______
- Introduction: Introduce yourself, explain why you are interested in the role
- Body: The goal of a cover letter is to talk about your experience and how you would be a good fit for this role and/or this company. This is where you do that.
- Closing: All the best, Your Full Name
