# User Scenario - Writing A Cover Letter

**Scenario**: 
User's can range from a person new to cooking, or someone experienced in the kitchen but looking to try something new or get out of their comfort zone.

User's are likely looking for instructions and a list of ingredients, a bonus would be any tips and tricks that they find. This recipe is not as intimidating as some, so the skill level of the audience will be wider.

Potential Scenario: A foodie is interested in recipes from the middle east, they go looking for recipes and happen upon the muhammara recipe. They decide to fix the recipe to go along with a few other recipes to make it a full meal. They serve it to their family along with pita and kabobs.
